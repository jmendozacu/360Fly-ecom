The apps that power your 360° world.

The 360fly mobile and desktop 360fly Director apps serve as your camera’s viewfinder, controller, editor, effects studio, library and more.

Miss nothing, share everything.

The 360fly apps make capturing, editing and sharing 360° video fun, easy and intuitive. They give you the power to share your best clips with family and friends across social media, directly from your device, with just the push of a button.

The 360fly
Android and iOS mobile app.

Use your mobile device as your camera’s viewfinder, controller and editor—then instantly share immersive 360° content, standard 16:9 edits of 360° footage and stills directly from your mobile device.

Discover the 360fly Director desktop app.

The 360fly Director desktop app has everything you need to edit, effect, organize and view your entire library of immersive, interactive 360° video on your personal computer.

Compatible iOS devices

360fly is compatible on iOS devices running iOS 9 or later.

Supported Devices:
Fully Compatible:

  • iPhone 5s
  • iPhone SE
  • iPhone 6/6s
  • iPhone 6Plus/6sPlus
  • iPhone 7,7Plus
  • iPad Pro
  • iPad Air
  • iPad Air 2
  • iPad Mini 2
  • iPad Mini 3
  • iPad Mini 4

Partially Compatible
The following devices are unable to offer full functionality, but they are able to explore videos from the site and have basic camera controls. However, they cannot play or edit videos that are on the camera. The reason for this is due to hardware limitations in the following devices, as 360fly apps require an A7 chipset or later.

  • iPhone 4S
  • iPhone 5
  • iPhone 5c
  • iPad (3rd generation)
  • iPad (4th generation)
  • iPad Mini

Compatible Android devices

Our 360fly Android app requires hardware with a 4k compatible display and Android Version 4.4 or later to function.

The following devices have all been tested by 360fly engineers:

  • HTC: M8, M9, A9
  • LG: G3, G4, Flex 2, Nexus 5
  • Motorola: Moto X 2nd Gen, Nexus 6, Droid Turbo/2, Moto Z
  • Samsung: Galaxy S5, Galaxy S6/Edge, Galaxy S7/Edge, Galaxy Note 4/Edge, Galaxy Note 5, Galaxy Note 7
  • Sony: Xperia Z3
  • Nexus 6P, Nexus 5X, Pixel

360fly Director System Requirements

To use our 360fly Director app, we have a few hardware and software requirements that your machine must meet or exceed:

Supported Operating Systems - Windows 7* and above, Mac OS X 10.8 and above
*64-bit OS required for 4K
*Microsoft Surface not supported at this time.
